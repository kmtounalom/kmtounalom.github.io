BUBBLE TEA

By: Kayla Tounalom

Bubble tea originated in Taiwan in 1980. Bubble tea consists of the sugar, milk, and tea. Bubble tea is enjoyed by many people and is quickly gaining popularity throughout the world. Originally bubble tea came from the bubble foam on the top and not the tapioca pearls. The foaming that appeared on top of the bubble tea was formed because of shaking. Originally Bubble tea was made with tea now there is a wide array of different flavor combinations.
